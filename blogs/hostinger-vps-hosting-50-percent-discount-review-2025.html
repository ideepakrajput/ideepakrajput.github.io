Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between KVM and OpenVZ virtualization?

KVM (Kernel-based Virtual Machine) provides full hardware virtualization, giving you a dedicated kernel and complete isolation from other users. OpenVZ uses container-based virtualization, which shares the host kernel and offers less isolation. KVM is generally more reliable and secure.
